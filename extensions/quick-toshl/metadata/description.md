# Quick Toshl

Add expenses, incomes, and transfers to your Toshl account directly from Raycast. View your recent transactions and monitor your budgets with ease.

## Features

- **Add Expense/Income/Transfer:** Quick entry forms for all your financial transactions.
- **Recent Transactions:** A detailed list of your latest activities.
- **Budgets:** Visual progress bars to help you stay on track with your spending.
- **Search Entries:** Advanced filtering to find exactly what you're looking for.
- **Planning:** View your spending plan and predictions.
- **AI Support:** Natural language processing for adding transactions (e.g., "$50 lunch today").
